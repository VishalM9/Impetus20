What? When? Why? Is an event which includes soft skills like decision making, logical reasoning, group discussion, interviews, etc. which help students improve their technical and soft skills.

ROUND 1:
Why should we? In round 1, each team will be provided with a question along with an answer but they need to specify reason Why should we or why we do it. Answer should be based on fact and be realistic, not imaginable. It will be a buzzer round.

ROUND 2:
When we are? In round 2, the qualiﬁed teams will have a face to face interview with the judges. Each team will be given a worksheet to solve the problem. Judges specify the problem statement where each team should give an answer to the problem statement along with Mathematically solved proof. Without the proof, any answer provided by the team will not be considered, the top 5 teams will be qualiﬁed for next round.

ROUND 3:
What went wrong? In round 3, 5 qualiﬁed teams will have an open discussion regarding the technological disasters. Teams need to specify what went wrong which led to the cause of disaster. Each team will be given a chance to speak. Before the discussion starts, a video will be displayed which speciﬁes how the disaster was overcome. Points will be scored based on the facts they speak regarding the topic.
